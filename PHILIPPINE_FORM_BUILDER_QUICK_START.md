## Troubleshooting

**Q: I don't see section grouping in questions list**
- A: Make sure "Philippine Exam Style" is toggled ON
- A: Ensure you have at least one section created

**Q: Questions not appearing under their sections**
- A: Edit the question and use "Assign to Section" dropdown
- A: Save the question - it should move to correct section

**Q: Can I change a question's section after creating it?**
- A: Yes! Click edit (âœï¸), change section dropdown, save

**Q: What happens to questions when I delete a section?**
- A: Questions move to "Unassigned Questions" area
- A: You can reassign them to another section or leave unassigned
